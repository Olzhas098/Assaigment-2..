Alita

Alita is the main character of the manga "Battle Angel Alita" and its film adaptation "Alita: Battle Angel". Alita is a cyborg with unique abilities and excellent combat skills, which she acquired as a result of being discovered in a dilapidated state by Dr. Dyson Ido in a garbage dump in the city of Zalito. Dr. Ido restored her body and gave her the name Alita.

Vector

Vector is the main villain of the manga "Battle Angel Alita" and its film adaptation "Alita: Battle Angel". He is the leader of the cybercrime world of the city of Zalito and is considered one of the most dangerous people in this world. Vector is a martial arts master and uses his skills to manage his gang of cybercriminals, gather information and eliminate his enemies. His ultimate goal is to achieve absolute power in the city of Zalito.

Dr. Dyson Ido

Dr. Dyson Ido is one of the main characters in the manga "Battle Angel Alita" and its film adaptation "Alita: Battle Angel". He is a great cybernetic surgeon specializing in the creation and restoration of cybernetic bodies.
